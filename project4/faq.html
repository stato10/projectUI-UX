Frequently Asked Questions



What is the age range for participants in the goal academy? The goal academy is open for participants aged between 7 and 18 years old.

What are the requirements to join the academy? Participants must have a love for the sport, be physically fit and bring their own soccer equipment. No prior experience is required.

How many training sessions are held each week? Our academy holds two training sessions per week, with each session lasting for 2 hours. These sessions are led by experienced coaches who aim to help participants improve their skills and develop their love for the sport.
